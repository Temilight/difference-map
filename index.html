Tools for understanding how you're wired.

Neurodivergence means your brain processes the world differently from what's considered typical. Autism, ADHD, dyslexia, dyspraxia, and more, all fall under it. It's a variation, not a flaw. These tools are built for neurodivergent people, and they come back to one idea: a lot of what feels like failure is actually a gap between what your day demands and what you actually have to give.
